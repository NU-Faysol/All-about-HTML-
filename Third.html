WHAT IS SINGLE PAIR EHTERNET?

SPE describes the transmission of Ethernet over only one pair of copper wires. In addition to data transmission via Ethernet, SPE also enables a simultaneous power supply of terminal devices via PoDL - Power over Data Line. Previously, this required two pairs for Fast Ethernet (100MB) and four pairs for Gigabit Ethernet. SPE now opens up completely new possibilities and fields of application for industrial Ethernet.
